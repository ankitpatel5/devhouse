Why live at the DevHouse?

Here are 4 big reasons...

Short Term

Looking for a short term place while you attend a coding bootcamp? We provide an affordable turn-key housing solution while you are in Austin.

No Worries

You have enough things to focus on, so let us do the heavy lifting here. The stay comes with all cable, utilities, gas, water, and bi-weekly cleaning service provided. You have literally nothing to worry about!

Good People

The people at the DevHouse will be attending one of the coding schools located in Austin. You will be surrounded by like-minded, passionate, and motivated individuals that you can learn from, bounce ideas off of each other, and create life long friendships as you venture through your coding journey.

Location, Location, Location!

We are located in east Austin, minutes away from downtown and many of the coding bootcamps, and close to all the Austin nightlife. Plenty of transportation options available.
